How to mark absentees
  1. 1. Open: carnival.up.railway.app/teacher
  2. 2. Log in with the teacher password.
  3. 3. Tap the Absentees tab.
  4. 4. Expand your class, tick absent students.
  5. 5. Tap Mark ticked ABSENT.
Why this matters
Absent students still have tokens in the system. If you don't mark them absent, someone could spend their tokens at a booth.
Deadline: 9:30 AM sharp. Booth operators log in 25 minutes after you.
