## For the teachers' group (send this first, with the PDF attached)

Hi all 👋 Quick carnival booth dry-run with your form class today — about 15 mins.

I've attached **booth-slides.pdf**. Open it on the classroom screen and skip to the slide for your class (3I1 to 3I7). Each teacher gets their own slide with three booth codes — please don't share the codes outside your class.

Walk the class through:
1. **Slide 1** — how to log in to a booth (URL: carnival.up.railway.app/booth)
2. **Slide 2** — what they'll see (search + pay)
3. **Your class slide** — your three booth codes (small, small, big)

Then split the class:
• A few students = booth operators (grab a tablet, type the code, log in)
• The rest = customers (walk up to a booth, let the operator search your name and tap red tokens to deduct)

I'll send each class's WhatsApp blurb in a separate message — feel free to forward it to your own class group after the run.

Any bugs or weirdness, ping me here. Thanks!
