How to log in to a booth

  1. 1. On your tablet, open:
    carnival.up.railway.app/booth
  2. 2. Type your booth code (4 digits) and tap Log in.
  3. 3. Search the customer's name or class.
  4. 4. Tap their name → tap red tokens → tap PAY.
  5. 5. Made a mistake? Press Undo within 60 seconds.
