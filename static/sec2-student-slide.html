CTSS Carnival · At a booth

YOU press PAY — not the operator

The operator sets the price. You confirm by tapping the green PAY button. This protects you from being over-charged.
How to play
  1. 1. Walk up to the booth. Tell the operator your name and class.
  2. 2. Operator searches and taps your name.
  3. 3. Operator taps red tokens to set the price. Check the number on the green button.
  4. 4. You tap the green PAY button.
  5. 5. If something looks wrong: ask the operator to press Cancel, or tell any teacher.
What you'll see
Red filled circles = tokens being deducted. The number on the green button = total cost. Check before you tap.
Over-charged by mistake? Find any teacher — they can refund tokens back to your account.
