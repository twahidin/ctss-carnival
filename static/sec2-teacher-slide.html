CTSS Carnival · Brief your form class

Tell your students: YOU press PAY

Booth operators cannot deduct tokens until the student taps PAY themselves. This protects students from being over-charged.
What students should do at a booth
  1. 1. Walk up to a booth. Tell the operator your name.
  2. 2. Operator searches and taps your name.
  3. 3. Operator taps red tokens to set the price. Check the number.
  4. 4. You tap the green PAY button — not the operator.
  5. 5. If something looks wrong: tell the operator to press Cancel, or ask any teacher to refund.
What they'll see
Red filled circles = tokens being deducted. Number on the green button = total cost.
If a student complains they were over-charged, look up their History on the teacher screen and use Refund to make it right.
